Caesar Salad

This is an awesome looking salad. I hope this brings you as much joy as the pain of seeing it at 2 AM.

Ingredients

Salad

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan Cheese

Caesar Dressing

  • Egg Yolk
  • Garlic
  • Lemon Juice
  • Olive Oil
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk Together
  2. Chop and Wash the Romaine
  3. Toss lettuce with dressing
  4. Add croutons and parmesan
  5. Top with chicken
