Somtam

This is an awesome looking somtam plate. I hope this brings you as much joy as the pain of seeing it at 2 AM.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups green papaya, shredded
  • 1–2 Thai chilies (adjust to taste)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar (or brown sugar)
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 8–10 long beans, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons roasted peanuts

Steps

  1. Peel and shred the green papaya into thin strips.
  2. In a mortar and pestle, pound the garlic and chilies until crushed.
  3. Add the palm sugar and mix until dissolved.
  4. Add the fish sauce and lime juice and stir to combine.
  5. Add the long beans and tomatoes and gently pound a few times to release their juices.
  6. Add the shredded papaya and toss everything together until well coated with the dressing.
  7. Sprinkle with roasted peanuts.
  8. Serve immediately.
